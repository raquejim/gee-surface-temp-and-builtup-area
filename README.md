# Surface Temperature and Builtup Area in Madrid 

The goal of this project is to investigate the relationship between surface temperature and percentage of built-up area in the city of Madrid through the use of Google Earth Engine. 

# Datasets used: 
1) global monthly daytime land surface temperature generated by the [Malaria Atlas Project at Oxford University](https://www.bdi.ox.ac.uk/research/malaria-atlas-project)
2) the global built-up area in the [Global Human Settlement Layer](https://human-settlement.emergency.copernicus.eu/ghs_bu2019.php) data generated by the European Commission
